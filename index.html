About us...

We are two Lancaster University students currently taking part in the CF:G beginners course. After some brainstorming we decided that this course would be a good oppurtunity to make a website advertising all the different places you could go for coffee and a catchup in the city. An ideal help guide for those who are new to the area or bored of the campus selection.
